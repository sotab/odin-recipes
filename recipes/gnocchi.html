Gnocchi

Description

Gnocchi is a popular italian dish that is easy to make at home thanks to the premade gnocchi pasta that you can buy in the store.

Ingredients

  • Gnocchi
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzarella chese
  • Tomato paste
  • Basil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan chese

Steps

  1. Cook the gnocchi in boiling water until they come to the surface of the water then take them out
  2. Add oil to the bottom of a baking pan and pour the gnocchis evenly into it
  3. Add the tomato paste on top of the gnocchis
  4. Cut the mozzarella and put the slices into the pan evenly
  5. Add some basil to it
  6. Add salt and pepper to your liking
    7. Bake it at 200 celcius degree for about 20 min or until the cheese fully melts Add Parmesan chese on top of it when served
