Lecsó

Description

Lecsó is a popular hungarian summer dish that is made from two key ingredients, paprika and tomato. Of course the original recepie contains sausage as well but you don't necesarrly have to use it if you don't eat meat.

The recepie is quite simple, as besides chopping vegetables you mainly have to stir the food a bit.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oil or fat (can be vegan)
  • Tomato
  • Green paprika
  • Paprika powder (sweet and smoked)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sausage (optional)

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Heat up fat in a pan and add the chopped onion to it, cook it until the onion is opaque.
  3. (Optional step) Add the chopped sausage before the onion is fully opaque.
  4. Add the tomato, paprika and the garlic to the pan and stir it until the tomato and paprika starts to release some liquid.
  5. Take the pan off from the heat and add sweet and smoked paprika powder to the dish and then put it back to the heat.
  6. Wait until the dish develops more liquid then put it to low heat.
  7. Cover the dish and stir every now and then for about 15-20 minutes
  8. Add salt and pepper to your liking
